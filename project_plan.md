# 🏈 Fantasy Football Front Office AI - Project Roadmap

## Vision

Build an AI-powered fantasy football front office that simulates the decision-making process of a professional NFL organization. Multiple AI executives collaborate using real fantasy football data to help fantasy managers make championship-winning decisions.

## 🎯 Version 1.0 Goal

A fantasy football owner can:

- Connect a Sleeper league
- View their real roster
- Receive AI recommendations from seven executives
- Get a President-approved weekly action plan
- Make waiver, lineup, and trade decisions using the AI front office

Target completion: July 2026
