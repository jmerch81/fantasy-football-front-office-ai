# Fantasy Football Front Office AI

## Product Vision

Fantasy Football Front Office AI is an AI-powered multi-agent decision intelligence platform for fantasy football owners.

The user is the Owner. Instead of using disconnected fantasy tools, the Owner receives recommendations from a full AI-powered front office.

## Core Experience

The app should feel like walking into a fantasy football team headquarters.

The Owner can open meetings, review reports, ask questions, evaluate trades, prepare for drafts, and receive strategic recommendations from specialized AI departments.

## AI Organization Chart

### Owner

The user. Makes final decisions.

### President of Football Operations

Coordinates the entire front office, gathers input from departments, resolves disagreements, and delivers final recommendations to the Owner.

### General Manager

Focuses on roster construction, trades, waivers, long-term strategy, and championship probability.

### Head Coach

Focuses on weekly lineup decisions, sit/start advice, matchups, and winning the current week.

### Draft War Room

Supports fantasy draft decisions using player rankings, roster needs, ADP value, upside, risk, and tier analysis.

### Director of Analytics

Focuses on projections, power rankings, expected points, consistency, ceiling, floor, boom/bust probability, and efficiency metrics.

### Director of Scouting

Finds sleepers, breakout candidates, undervalued players, rookies, and player similarity insights.

### Sports Medicine Department

Tracks injury status, practice participation, reinjury risk, workload, snap counts, and availability.

### Football Intelligence Department

Owns external game context including weather, stadium type, wind, rain, temperature, travel, time zones, short weeks, altitude, and schedule difficulty.

### Trade Committee

Evaluates trades from multiple perspectives and grades short-term, long-term, and championship impact.

### AI Owner Chat

Allows the Owner to ask questions and call meetings.

## Weekly Rhythm

### Tuesday Morning: Owner's Report

Released after Monday Night Football is complete.

Includes:

- Weekly result
- Record
- League standing
- Power ranking
- Playoff odds
- Championship odds
- Roster strengths
- Roster weaknesses
- Waiver priorities
- Trade recommendations
- GM grade
- Coaching grade
- Analytics grade
- Medical grade

### Wednesday: Waiver Intelligence

Identifies priority waiver claims, FAAB recommendations, replacement options, and roster upgrade opportunities.

### Thursday: Game Planning Report

Initial lineup recommendation, matchup analysis, and week-specific strategy.

### Friday: Medical and Weather Report

Injury updates, practice status, weather risk, player availability, and snap count concerns.

### Saturday: Final Coaching Meeting

Final lineup recommendation before Sunday games.

### Sunday Morning: Command Center

Inactive player updates, breaking news, weather changes, and final substitutions.

### Monday Night: Live Win Probability

Tracks remaining players, points needed, and matchup odds.

## Signature Feature: Situation Room

When an important event happens, the President of Football Operations activates the Situation Room.

Examples:

- Major injury
- Trade offer
- Draft pick on the clock
- Playoff matchup
- Waiver deadline
- Last-minute inactive player

Each department presents its view. The departments may agree or disagree. The President synthesizes the input and gives the Owner a final recommendation.
